Water Prefab consist of three elements.

From bottom up:

Firts is the Water Circle 01_PTX that has the POLYTEX - Paradise Water Mat1 material.
This layer only has the material and doesnt have collision.

Second is the Collider that prevents the Character from falling down.

Third is the Swim Collider Circle. This Collider tells the Character Controller script
that the player is swimming. It is set up Tag "Water".
